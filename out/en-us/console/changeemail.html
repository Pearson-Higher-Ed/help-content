Can I change my name, username, or email address?

From the My Account page, you can change:

  • Last Name
  • Email Address

You, your instructor, and Pearson Support cannot change:

  • First Name
  • Username

If you 'd like your email address and username to be the same, you can edit your email address to match your username.

To manage your account settings:

  1. Sign into your course.
  2. From the toolbar and in the drop-down by your name, select My Account.
  3. Click Edit beside the fields provided to update your account information accordingly.
  4. Click Save.
