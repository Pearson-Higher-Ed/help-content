How long before my account is validated?

It can take up to one business day (24 hours) to validate a new REVEL instructor. You will receive an email notification when your account has been validated. If you don't receive an email after one business day, please contact your Sales Rep to check the status of your request.

Keep in mind, you can create a course before your instructor status is verified. Use the Look for Materials link to search for course titles and click Create Course to start adding courses. Once your instructor account is verified, you'll have access to the courses you've created.
