Creating a course?

Don't forget to confirm your email address and validate your instructor status so you can access your course after you create it.

To create a course:

  1. From My Courses, click Search for Materials.
  2. Browse our inventory of digital materials by:
    • Performing a genearal search
    • Browsing materials by a specific subject or area of study
    • Looking at our new materials and what's trending
  3. In your search results, select the title of interest to learn more about what's inside.
  4. Click CREATE COURSE.
  5. Complete the form provided with your course information accordingly. Remember, the course name you enter is the course name students will see on their My Courses page.
  6. Click Save. Your course displays on the My Courses page.
