Creating a course?

Don't forget to confirm your email address and validate your instructor status so you can access your course after you create it. You will see these prompts in the Account Settings form.

To create a course:

  1. From Course Materials, select Search for Materials.
  2. Browse our inventory of digital materials by:
    • Performing a general search
    • Browsing materials by a specific subject or area of study
    • Looking at our new materials and what's trending
  3. In your search results, select the title of interest to learn more about what's inside.
  4. While the course title you want to use is open, access the form to create or set up your course. For example, for Pearson eText, select Set It Up.

  5. Complete the form provided with your course information.

    Tip: Students will see the course name you enter when they access their My Courses page. You can always edit this later from the Settings page for the course.

  6. Select Save. Your course displays on your Course Materials page.
