Why can't I access my courses? I requested access at least one day ago.

The email address, last name, and first name you entered on the Educator Request Access form may not exactly match the information you entered when you created your instructor account. Return to the Educator Request Access form and confirm your information matches.

To confirm your instructor account:

  1. Click Get Access to View Course.
  2. Proceed to request form.
  3. Skim the form for what's auto-entered in the First name, Last name and Email address fields. To ensure this information matches your instructor account for the course, do not alter the name or email address information.
  4. Click Submit Your Request once you've completed all fields.
