Why can't I access my courses? I requested access at least one day ago.

The email address, last name, and first name you entered on the Request Instructor Access form may not exactly match the information you entered when you created your instructor account. Return to the Request Instructor Access form and confirm your information matches.

To confirm your instructor account:

  1. From My Account, click Account settings.
  2. From the Instructor access field, click Request instructor access.
  3. Check the First name, Last name, and Email address fields to ensure this information matches your instructor account for the course. Do not change the name or email address information.
  4. After you've completed all fields, click Submit Your Request.
