How do I change my course settings? Will this affect assignment dates?

If you change your course start or end dates, remember to also adjust the start or due dates for the assignments in your course. Course settings do not synchronize with course assignments.

To change course settings:

  1. From Course Materials, click the down arrow for the course you want to change.
  2. Click Settings.
  3. Change your course settings as needed.
  4. Click Save.
  5. In the toolbar, click the Pearson logo to return to the course list.

When you're ready to adjust assignment due dates, click to open your course and select Assignments to access your assignment information.
