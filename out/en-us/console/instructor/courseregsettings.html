How do I change my Course Registration settings? Will this impact assignment dates?

If you change your course start or end dates, remember to also adjust the start or due dates to the assignments in your course. Course registration settings do not auto-synchronize with course assignments.

To change course registration settings:

  1. Click Course Options for the course in which you want to change settings.
  2. Click Settings.
  3. Change your course settings accordingly.
  4. Click Save.
  5. In the toolbar, click the Pearson logo to return to the course list.
