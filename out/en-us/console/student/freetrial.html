Can I extend my temporary access?

Temporary access cannot be extended. After your temporary access period ends, when you try to access your course materials, you are prompted to do any of the following:

  • Purchase and enter a prepaid product access code purchased from the campus bookstore
  • Pay by credit card (or PayPal)

Note: The day you receive the invitation link from your instructor might not be the actual course start date.

Tip: Getting your course materials as soon as you receive your invitation link gives you time to get settled in and explore.
