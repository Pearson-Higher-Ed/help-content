Why was I dropped from my course?

Pearson offers full refunds for online purchases made only through Pearson.

Why was I dropped from my course?

  • If you were accidentally dropped from your course, contact your instructor. Your instructor can reactivate your enrollment.
  • If you transferred to another section of a course OR signed up for the wrong section, instructor, or both, contact your new instructor. If your new course is using the same materials, your instructor can send another invitation link and you can access your materials without having to make another purchase or redeem another access code.

How can I get a refund?

Pearson offers full refunds for online purchases made only through Pearson. You must request a refund within 15 days of purchase. See the instructions below for your product.

Note: Pearson Support cannot process refund requests.

What happens after I've requested my refund?

If you purchased the course from Pearson, you will receive two emails from webaccesscs@pearson.com letting you know the status of your refund request. To ensure that you receive these emails, add this account as a trusted address (or check your spam folder).
