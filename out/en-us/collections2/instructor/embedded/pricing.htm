What Does it Cost to Print Uploaded Content?

Uploaded content costs vary, depending on the size of the Collection. In general, the cost is the Collection's base price plus a per page cost for uploaded content over 50 pages.

The base price is $8 (for any Collection containing only original uploaded material) or $15 (for any Collection with a mix of Pearson content and uploaded material).

The per page cost is $0 for the first fifty pages of uploaded content. For example, a 50 page custom book (created from a print Collection) containing all original uploaded content costs $8. The same book with Pearson content replacing one chapter costs $15.

The cost for every page of uploaded material over 50 in the printed book is:

  • 2 cents (black and white pages)
  • 6 cents (color pages)

Note: If you use a mix of color and black and white pages, you are charged 6 cents for every page in the book over 50 pages.

Examples

  • A 300 page book (Collection) of original uploaded black and white content costs: $8 + (250 x 0.02) = $13.
  • A 300 page book of original uploaded color content costs: $8 + (250 x 0.06) = $23.
  • A 300 page book of original uploaded content, where some chapters are black and white and some are color costs: $8 + (250 x 0.06) = $23.
  • A 300 page book of Pearson and uploaded black and white content costs: $15 + (250 x 0.02) = $20.
  • A 300 page book of Pearson and uploaded color content costs $15 + (250 x 0.06) = $30.
  • A 300 page book of Pearson and uploaded content, where some chapters are black and white and some are color costs: $15 + (250 x 0.06) = $30.
