What does this status mean?

Your Collection includes content that is out-of-print or the rights to the content are no longer owned by Pearson. As a result, the Collection cannot by printed or ordered with the current ISBN.

To correct this, you can copy the Collection with the content that is still available and submit it to obtain a new ISBN.
