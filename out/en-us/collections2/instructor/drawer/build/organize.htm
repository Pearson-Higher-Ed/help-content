How do I organize my content?

Move and group the content into a logical order using one of these methods:

  • Select the items to move and drag and drop them to a new location. This works best when you need to move the content a short distance, for example transposing two items.
  • Select Option (the button with three dots) and then one of the move selections, for example, Move to Top or Move to Bottom.
  • If you've grouped content into parts, select Option, and then select one of the Move to options. For example, move an item into another part, select Move to another part and then select the part for the selected item.

You can group content together by creating a part and moving content into it. The part is not a container. It is a logical divider for you to organize content.

  1. Place the mouse pointer over the divider that separates two items on the screen. The text Create a Part appears.
  2. Select Create a Part. The new part appears for you to rename and move content into.
