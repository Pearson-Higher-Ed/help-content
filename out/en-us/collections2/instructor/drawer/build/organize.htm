How do I organize my content?

Move and group the content into a logical order using one of these methods:

  • Select the items to move and drag and drop them to a new location. Use this method to move content a short distance, for example transposing two items.
  • Select Option (the button with three dots) and then one of the Move commands. Use Move to Position to move an item to a specific location. For example, to move a chapter to position 5, increment the value in the field to 5. If you've grouped content into parts, Use Move to another part to move content from one part to another.

You can group content together by creating a part and moving content into it. The part is not a container. It is a logical divider for you to organize content.

  1. Place the mouse pointer over the divider that separates two items on the screen. The text Create a Part appears.
  2. Select Create a Part. The new part appears for you to rename and move content into.
