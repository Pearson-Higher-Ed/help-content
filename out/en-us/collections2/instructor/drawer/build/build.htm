How Do I Build a Collection?

  1. Add content to the Collection by searching for or uploading the content you need.

    Tip: Name the Collection by editing the default text. Also spend time organizing the content too. It will improve the overall quality of the custom book.

  2. For a print Collection, select a cover image, define a title page, and select the print options for your book.
  3. Supply course information for the course that will use the Collection.
  4. Review and submit the Collection for approval. Once approved, the Collection is printed as a custom book or available digitally for students to use.

If you are having trouble building a Collection, you can request help. Specify in the field at the bottom of the form whether you want help with a print or digital Collection.
