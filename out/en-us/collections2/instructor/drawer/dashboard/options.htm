Collection Options

Depending on the status of your Collection, you will have one or more options.

In progress Collections:

    Edit: Lets you modify the Collection

    View: Lets you preview the Collection

    Share: Lets you share the Collection with another person

    Copy: Lets you duplicate the Collection

    Request Assistance: Lets you request assistance building the Collection

    Delete: Lets you delete the Collection

    Remove: Lets you remove a Collection that has been shared with you from the screen. The shared Collection is not changed in any way and other users with whom it has been shared with will still have access to it.

Published Collection: Same options except for Edit.

No Longer Available: Contains out-of-print material or Pearson no longer has rights to some or all of the content. Your only options are:

    View the Table of Contents: Displays the TOC so you can determine which content is out-of-print

    Copy Available Content: Replicates any in-print content to another Collection so you can edit and republish it
