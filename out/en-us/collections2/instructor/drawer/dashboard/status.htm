Collection Statuses

Collections you create always have one of the following statuses:

In progress: The Collection is under development; you can edit an in-progress Collection until you are ready to publish it.

Submitted: The Collection has been submitted for publication.

No Longer Available: Your Collection includes content that is out-of-print or the rights to the content are no longer owned by Pearson. As a result, the Collection cannot be printed or ordered with the current ISBN.

To correct this, you can copy the Collection with the content that is still available and submit it to obtain a new ISBN.
