How Do I Preview a Collection?

Place the mouse pointer over the image in the Collection's tile and select Preview Collection. You can then show the Collection's Table of Contents (TOC) or any bookmarks that have been made, and search the Collection for specific content.

To preview content from the Table of Contents, select the item's title.

To bookmark an item, click the bookmark at the top of the screen.

To view the TOC or the available bookmarks, open the left tray and click Contents or Bookmarks, depending on want you want to show.

To page through a text-based resource:

  • Enter the page number you want to see in the Page field at the bottom of the screen.
  • Click in the Page field to view the arrow buttons; click the top arrow to move to the next page and click the bottom arrow to move to the previous page.
  • Use the arrow keys across the left and right edges of the screen.

To change the magnification of a resource, select a magnification percentage from the list. You can also zoom in and out one level of magnification by clicking the plus and minus buttons.
