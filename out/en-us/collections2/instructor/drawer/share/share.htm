How Do I Share a Collection?

You can share a Collection with one or more people by entering the email addresses of the recipients one at a time and pressing Enter.

Pearson Collections imposes no specific work flow on when to share a Collection. You can share it after you initially create it, after you make a pass for relevant content, or just before the Collection is ready to be published.

You have two options when sharing a Collection:

  • Share an entire Collection: This option requires anyone receiving the content to have a Pearson profile.
  • Share only the cover and Table of Contents: This option requires anyone receiving the files have an email address. Only print Collections have this option.

Important: Publishing a shared Collection publishes it for all users and prevents subsequent changes. So if you are sharing a Collection to garner feedback, do so before you publish it.

To share a Collection:

  1. Select the share option you'd like to use.
  2. Enter the email address of the person with whom you want to share the Collection in field provided. Be sure to press Enter to complete your entry.
  3. (Optional) Enter any notes or a message you want to include, and click Share.

To share the Collection with multiple people, repeat step 2, entering each email address one at a time. Be sure to press Enter to complete your entry.

To stop sharing a Collection with an individual, select the x next to the email address of that person.
