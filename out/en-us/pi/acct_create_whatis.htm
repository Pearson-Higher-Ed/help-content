What can I do with my new Pearson account?

Your Pearson account consists of your username, password, and other personal information.

Use your new Pearson account to register for other Pearson courses, including courses integrated with a Learning Management System.

Using one Pearson account saves you time! You don't have to enter your personal information again and only have one username and password to remember.
