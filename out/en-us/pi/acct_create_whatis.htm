What else can I do with my new Pearson account?

Your Pearson account consists of your username and password along with other personal information. To access REVEL from Blackboard, you only need to link your Pearson and Blackboard accounts once. You can also use your new Pearson account to register for access to other Pearson courses outside of Blackboard. When you sign in with your account during registration, your new course access gets added to your existing account. For example, if you use your account to register for a MyLab product, you can use the same account to register for a Modified Mastering product.

Using one Pearson account saves you time! You don't have to enter your personal information again and only have one username and password to remember.
