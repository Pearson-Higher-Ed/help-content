Having trouble signing in or account locked?

You have a Pearson username and password if you've ever used a Pearson product (for example, REVEL, MyMathLab, or MasteringBiology). Use an existing account to save time and only have one username and password to remember.

Instead of creating a new account, try these tips for resolving common issues with signing in:

Username: Your username might be your email address and differ from your school or institution username. Username is called login name on some Pearson websites.

Password: Your password is case-sensitive and has at least one letter, one number, and eight characters. Select SHOW to see your password as you enter it.

Get username and reset password: Select Forgot username or password to get an email with your username and a link to reset your password. If you have more than one Pearson account associated with your email address, the email has multiple usernames and reset password links.

Incorrect username or password or account locked: If a message says your username or password is incorrect and you think you entered them correctly or a message says your account is locked, select Forgot username or password. Reset your password and then sign in.

Browser cookies: Make sure your browser cookies are enabled or cleared.

If you still can't sign in, contact Pearson Support.
