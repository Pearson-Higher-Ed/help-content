Any username or password tips?

You have a Pearson username and password if you've used a Pearson product (for example, REVEL, MyMathLab, or MasteringBiology) in the past.

Username

Might differ from your school or institution username.

​Might be your email address.

Is called login name on some Pearson websites.

Password

Is case-sensitive.

Has at least one letter, one number, and eight characters.

Select SHOW to see your password to make sure you enter it correctly.

Forgot username or password

Select Forgot username or password to get an email with your username and a link to reset your password.

If you have more than one Pearson account associated with your email address, the email will contain multiple usernames and reset password links.

Tip: If you entered the correct username and password and have trouble signing in or linking accounts, make sure your browser cookies are enabled or cleared before you contact Pearson Support.
