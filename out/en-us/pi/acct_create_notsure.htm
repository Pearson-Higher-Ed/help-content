What should I do if I remember that I have a Pearson account?

From the Create an account page:

  • You can click Link Accounts at the bottom of the page if you remember your Pearson account. You'll return to the Link Accounts page where you enter your username and password.
  • If you enter an email address associated with an existing account, a message appears asking you to sign in, look up your account information, or continue creating the new account.

We recommend you use an existing account to get access to REVEL, so you don't have to enter your personal information again and only have one username and password to remember.
