What should I do if I remember that I have a Pearson account?

From the Create an account page:

  • Select Link Accounts to enter your username and password.
  • If you enter an email address associated with an existing Pearson account, a popup appears enabling you to look up your account information or continue creating the new account.

Save time by using an existing account!
