Do I have a Pearson account?

If you've ever used a Pearson product, you probably have an account. We recommend you use an existing account instead of creating a new one. This saves you time, and you only have one username and password to remember.

To get your account information, click Forgot your username or password and follow the instructions. If you have an account, you'll get an email with your username and a link to reset your password. (Tip: The Reset Password page opens in a separate window or tab from where you started. After you reset your password, close both windows or tabs and return to Blackboard. Navigate to the Go to REVEL link to continue.)

Your username and password are case-sensitive.

Important: Make sure you only link one account to your Pearson account. Otherwise, there might be a problem with grade return from Pearson REVEL to Blackboard.
