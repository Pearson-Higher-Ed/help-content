What happens after I request my username and password?

If you have a Pearson account, you'll get an email or see the Reset your password page with your username and a link to reset your password. For multiple accounts, all your usernames and separate links to reset your passwords are listed.

If you don't get a reset link, try again with a different email address or mobile number.

Note: If you're linking accounts from a Learning Management System (LMS), the Reset Password page opens in a new window or tab. After you reset your password, close both windows or tabs and return to your LMS to continue.
