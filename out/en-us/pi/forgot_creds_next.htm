What happens after I request my username and password?

If you have a Pearson account, you'll get an email with your username and a link to reset your password. For multiple accounts, the email lists all your usernames and separate links to reset your passwords.

If you don't get an email, follow the tips from the message you get after requesting to reset your password.

Note: If you're linking accounts from a Learning Management System (LMS), the Reset Password page opens in a new window or tab. After you reset your password, close both windows or tabs and return to your LMS to continue.
