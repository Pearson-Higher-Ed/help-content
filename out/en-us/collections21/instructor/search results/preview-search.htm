How Do I Preview from Search?

You can preview searched-for content. Previewing content lets you interact with and view it the way your students will. Select an item's title to preview it.

Note: To preview a Book, you first view its Table of Contents and then you select the chapter to preview.

To page through a text-based resource:

  • Enter the page number you want to see in the Page field at the bottom of the screen.
  • Click in the Page field to view the arrow buttons; click the top arrow to move to the next page and click the bottom arrow to move to the previous page.
  • Use the arrow keys across the left and right edges of the screen.

To change the magnification of a resource, select a magnification percentage from the list. You can also zoom in and out one level of magnification by clicking the plus and minus buttons.
