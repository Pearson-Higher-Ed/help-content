How Do I Preview my Collection in Progress?

Click Preview from the Build screen. The arrangement of content in the Collection acts as the Collection's Table of Contents (TOC). You also see any bookmarks you've made and can search the Collection.

To preview content from the Table of Contents, select the item's title.

To bookmark an item, click the bookmark at the top of the screen.

To view the TOC or the available bookmarks, open the left tray and click Contents or Bookmarks.

To page through a text-based resource:

  • Enter the page number you want to view in the Page field at the bottom of the screen.
  • Click the Page field to view the arrow buttons; use the top arrow to move to the next page and the bottom arrow to move to the previous page.
  • Use the arrow keys on the left and right edges of the screen.

To change the magnification of a resource, select a magnification percentage from the list. You can also zoom in and out one level of magnification by clicking the plus and minus buttons.
