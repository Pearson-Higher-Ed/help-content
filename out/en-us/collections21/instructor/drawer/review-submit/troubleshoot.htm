Why Can't I Submit my Collection?

The Review and Submit screen shows any errors or conditions that will cause issues with printing your Collection. You must resolve these issues before you can submit your Collection for publication. Common issues include:

  • Too much content in the Collection. The number of pages in a Collection cannot exceed 1200 pages.
  • Not enough content in the Collection. You must have at least 100 pages of content in your Collection before it can be printed.
  • Not having the right mix of content. Certain content is subject to restricted-use agreements under which you must have at least 3 or 4 resources of the same type. Pearson may also lose the rights to content between the time you add it to your Collection and review it for publication. If any of these conditions apply, you will see messages instructing you what to add or remove.
  • Not supplying course information. You must complete the Course Information screen before you can submit your Collection.
  • Entering a class size of 9 or less on the Course Information screen. Collections supports class sizes of 10 or more.
  • Not completing the Cover and Printing screens for printed Collections. You must select a cover image, define a title page, and set the available print options before your Collection can be printed.
