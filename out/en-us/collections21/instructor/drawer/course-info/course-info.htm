Supplying Course Information

You need to supply information about your course when you submit your Collection. This helps us prioritize your Collection's approval.

You can enter the information any time before you submit the Collection. If you forget to supply the information, Collections displays a message to remind you. To enter course information.

  1. Go to the Build screen and click Course Information.
  2. Enter the information requested.
  3. Verify any information filled in for you. Make any changes using Change. If the information is not displayed, you can search for it by name or email, or add it manually.
  4. Verify the location where the class will be taught and make any modifications using Change. If this information is not displayed, enter it.
  5. (print Collections only) If you need to bundle your Collection with other Pearson books or access cards, enter the ISBN of each item. To add additional fields, click +.
