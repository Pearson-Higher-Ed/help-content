Am I signed in to REVEL?

After linking your Pearson and Canvas accounts, you can access REVEL content anytime without linking accounts again. Each time you access REVEL from Canvas, you're automatically signed into REVEL.

For security: Make sure you always sign out of REVEL before you close the REVEL window or tab, especially if you share your laptop or other device.
