How do I manage my REVEL content and tools from Blackboard?

Use the Pearson REVEL tools page to:

  • Access REVEL content.
  • Manage the Pearson student roster.
  • Refresh Pearson grades in the Blackboard Grade Center.
  • View and export information to provide Pearson Support to troubleshoot issues.
  • View Pearson REVEL Help & Support for Blackboard.
