How do I find out whether I have a Pearson account?

If you've ever used a Pearson product, you probably have an account. We recommend you use an existing account to get access to REVEL instead of creating a new one. That way, you don't have to enter your personal information again and only have one username and password to remember.

To get your account information, click Forgot your username or password and follow the instructions. If you have an account, you'll get an email with your username and a link to reset your password.
