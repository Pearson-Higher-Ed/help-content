Should I purchase an online subscription? 

We've compiled a list of our more frequently asked questions about purchasing an online subscription. Here's what students are asking:

What's included with a subscription (tests, homework, flashcards, etc.)?

Whether you purchase an access code at your school bookstore or opt for the online purchase with immediate access using a credit card or PayPal account, you receive access to everything your Pearson digital text materials have to offer! Your complete course text, videos, interactives, quizzing, highlight and notetaking functionality, an assignment calendar, and performance dashboard -- it’s all yours!

How long can I use this product for?

Varied by product, the time period is listed in your product's purchase options. You will have access to your digital course materials for the time period specified from the point of purchase.

Do I need an access code?

No, by clicking Purchase Now, you can buy instant access to your digital materials and you will not need an access code.
