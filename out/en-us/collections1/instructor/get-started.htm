Create a Collection That Works for You

Your course materials should match your course, not the other way around. Collections makes this possible with its robust catalog linked to easy-to-use curation tools. Everything is set-up so you can easily design your course library and then share it with your students. It's as easy as 1-2-3.

1. Select Your Delivery Method

Choose whether you want to deliver your Collection to students as a printed book or give them digital access. You can also do both.

2. Choose Your Content

Contact Sales to build a Collection for you or Do-it-yourself with pre-curated Collections.

You can begin with Collection curated by subject matter experts or do it yourself from scratch to give your students the exact mix of content you want to cover.

Select course materials from your favorite Pearson digital books, cases, readings, and primary sources. You can also upload content you've created and own.

3. Publish Your Collection

After you're done assembling your Collection, it's time to get it into your students' hands. Publish your project, and we'll take it from there. For print Collections, you will receive an ISBN immediately upon publication so that you can place the order with your bookstore. Printed collections are manufactured on demand and shipped to your bookstore within 3 weeks of placing the order.

Digital collections will be ready for student registration and use within a few days. We'll notify you and provide a web link that takes students directly to your Collection.
