Can I change my name, username, or email address?

Tip: If you 'd like your email address and username to be the same, you can edit your email address to match your username.

To manage your account settings:

  1. Sign into your course.
  2. From the My Account drop-down by your name, select Account settings.

  3. To update your account information, select Edit.

    You, your instructor, and Pearson Support cannot edit your first name.

  4. Click Save.
