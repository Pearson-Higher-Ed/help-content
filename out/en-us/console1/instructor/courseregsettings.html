How do I change my course settings? Will this affect assignment dates?

Tip: If you change the start or end dates in your course settings, remember to also adjust the start or due dates for your scheduled assignments or readings. (Course settings do not synchronize with scheduled assignments or readings.)

To change course settings:

  1. From Course Materials, select the down arrow for the course you want to change.
  2. Select Settings.
  3. Change your course settings, as needed.
  4. Select Save.
  5. To return to the course list, select the Pearson logo.

When you're ready to adjust due dates, open your course and make any needed changes.
