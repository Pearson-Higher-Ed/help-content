Why can't I access my courses? I requested access at least one day ago.

It's possible that the email address and last name you entered on the Request Instructor Access form do not match the information you entered when you originally created your instructor account. Return to the Request Instructor Access form and confirm that it matches your account information.

To confirm your instructor account:

  1. From My Account, select Account settings.
  2. From the Instructor access field, select Request instructor access.
  3. Check the Last name and Email address fields to ensure this information matches your instructor account for the course. If this information does not match, update the Account Settings form.
  4. After you've updated your account settings, select Save.
