What can I do here?

On the Pearson Revel page, you can:

  • Access content by selecting the Revel image or Open Revel from the Options menu.
  • Under Grade Sync, choose to sync grades from Revel to your Canvas gradebook. You can sync overall (total) scores or select assignments to sync automatically when available or anytime. (Before you change grade settings, make sure you're the only instructor doing so and grades aren't currently being synced.)

    To see recent syncs, select Recent Activity from the Options menu for Grade Sync.

  • Under Help & Support, access instructor or student Help, Pearson Support, or download diagnostics to give support for troubleshooting issues.
