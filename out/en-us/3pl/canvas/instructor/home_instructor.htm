What can I do here?

On the Pearson REVEL page, you can:

  • Select Add Content to search for and select content when you first access REVEL from your Canvas course. If you already added content, select Open REVEL.
  • Under Grade Sync, choose to sync grades from REVEL to your Canvas gradebook automatically as they become available or anytime on request, and sync assignments or total score. (Before you change grade settings, make sure you're the only instructor doing so and grades aren't currently being synced.)
  • Under Help & Support, access instructor or student help, Pearson Support, or diagnostics to give support for troubleshooting issues.
