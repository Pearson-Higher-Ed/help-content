When do I link my accounts?

The first time you access REVEL, you permanently link your Pearson and Canvas accounts. You only need to do this once for all your Canvas courses that use REVEL content. Next time you access REVEL from Canvas, you go straight to your content.

If you finish linking your accounts and close the browser or tab before going to the next step, your accounts remain linked when you try again.
