What is the course name?

Your REVEL content must have a course name that appears if you or your students access your content directly from REVEL.

In most cases, the course name from Blackboard automatically appears in the box. You can use the default or enter a different course name.

The course name doesn't have to be unique and can be different from the Blackboard course name.
