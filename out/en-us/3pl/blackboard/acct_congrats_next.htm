What's next after linking accounts?

After linking your Pearson and Blackboard accounts:

Students: Purchase access to REVEL content using an access code, or credit card or PayPal (U.S. only). A free trial is also available.

Instructors: Search for REVEL content to add to your Blackboard course.

If you close the browser or tab before going to the next step, you won't need to link your accounts again.
