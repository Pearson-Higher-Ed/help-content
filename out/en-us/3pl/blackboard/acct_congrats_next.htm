What happens after I link my accounts?

After linking your Pearson and Blackboard accounts:

  • Students: Purchase access to REVEL content using an access code, or credit card or PayPal (U.S. only). Free temporary access is also available.
  • Instructors: Search for and select REVEL content to add to your Blackboard course.

If you close the browser or tab before going to the next step, you don't need to link your accounts again.
