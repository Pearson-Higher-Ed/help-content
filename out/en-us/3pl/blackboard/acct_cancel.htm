How do I cancel linking my accounts?

If you decide not to continue linking your Pearson and Blackboard accounts, you can close the browser window or tab. A message appears saying your Pearson account was not linked. When you want to continue linking your accounts, return to Blackboard and select Go to Pearson REVEL from the Content page.
