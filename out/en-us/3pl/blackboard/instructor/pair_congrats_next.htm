What's next after I select Return to Course?

When you select Return to Course, the Pearson Confirmation page in Blackboard appears for you to choose the folder location of the Pearson link. You can then access the Pearson content and tools from your Blackboard course.

If you don't select Return to Course, the next time you access Pearson, the Pearson Confirmation page appears before your content.
