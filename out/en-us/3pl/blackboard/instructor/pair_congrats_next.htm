Where does Return to Course take me?

To finish adding REVEL content to your Blackboard course, you must select Return to Course.

The Pearson REVEL Confirmation page in Blackboard appears for you to choose the folder location of your REVEL link. You can then go to REVEL and access REVEL tools from your Blackboard course.

Important: If you skip these steps, REVEL content isn't added to your Blackboard course and you need to start again.
