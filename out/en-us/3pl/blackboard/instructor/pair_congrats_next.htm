Where does Return to Course take me?

When you select Return to Course, the Pearson REVEL Confirmation page in Blackboard appears for you to choose the folder location of your REVEL link. You can then access your REVEL content and tools from your Blackboard course.

If you don't select Return to Course, the next time you access REVEL, the Pearson REVEL Confirmation page appears before your content.
