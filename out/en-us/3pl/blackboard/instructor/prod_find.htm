How do I find my content?

After you link your accounts, you search for REVEL content to add to your course. Note that only REVEL content, not content associated with MyLab & Mastering, is available.

You can enter specific search terms, such as a title or ISBN. To broaden your search and browse more content, enter a subject keyword or author name.

To get a list of content results, enter your search terms and click the blue magnifying glass.
