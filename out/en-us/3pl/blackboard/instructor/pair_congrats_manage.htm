How do I manage my REVEL course and content from Blackboard?

You can go to the Pearson REVEL Tools page to work with your course and content, including:

  • Manage the Pearson student roster.
  • Refresh Pearson grades in the Blackboard Grade Center.
  • View and export information to provide Pearson 24/7 Support to troubleshoot issues.
  • View Pearson REVEL Help & Support for Blackboard.
