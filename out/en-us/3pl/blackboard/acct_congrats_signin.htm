Am I signed in to REVEL?

After you link your Pearson and Blackboard accounts, you can access your REVEL content anytime without linking accounts again. Each time you access REVEL from Blackboard, you are automatically signed into REVEL.

Important: For security, make sure you always sign out of REVEL before you close the REVEL window or tab, especially if you are sharing your laptop or other device.
