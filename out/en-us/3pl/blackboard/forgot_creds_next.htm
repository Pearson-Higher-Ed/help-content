What happens after I submit my email address to get my username and password?

If you have a Pearson account, you'll get an email with your username and a link to reset your password.

For multiple accounts, the email lists all your usernames and separate links to reset your passwords. If you used a Pearson account to access REVEL in the past, we recommended you use the same account to access your current REVEL content.
