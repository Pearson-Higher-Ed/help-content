Grade sync

If available, you can sync grades from your Pearson content to your Brightspace course.

Set up grade sync anytime. Make sure at least one student who registered and linked accounts starts at least one assignment.

Under Grade Sync, you can:

  • Turn automatic sync on or off. This syncs grades as they become available without taking any action. (You can still select Sync Now to sync grades anytime.)
  • Sync grades now for all assignments already available or that became available to students since you last synced grades.
  • Sync all or selected assignments or overall score. (Depending on your Pearson product, you may not see the Assignments option.)
