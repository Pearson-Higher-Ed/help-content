Do I have a Pearson account?

If you've ever used a Pearson product, you probably have an account. We recommend you use an existing account instead of creating a new one. This saves you time, and you only have one username and password to remember.

To get your account information, select Forgot your username or password and follow the instructions. If you have an account, you'll get an email with your username and a link to reset your password. (If you're linking accounts from a Learning Management System, the Reset Password page opens in a new window or tab. After you reset your password, close both windows or tabs and return to your Learning Management System to continue.)

Your username and password are case-sensitive.
